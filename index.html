About Us

Established in 1989, in the origins of Cape Town,South Africa. We wanted to make a difference in the sandwich making community giving affordable prices to middle-class income people. We make our products from a range of natural ingredients fresh from local farmns. We also donate most of our products that were not bought to local helping shelters. 20% Percent of our income goes to charity to help the less fortunate. We aim to give our clients nthe best and most delicious experience.
